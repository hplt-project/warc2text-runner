At the 2012 MTV Movie Awards on Sunday night, Christian Bale, Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Gary Oldman took the stage to introduce an exclusive sneak preview of the next installment in Christopher Nolan‘s legendary Batman franchise.
The one-and-a-half minutes of footage showcase the new Batmobiles and Catwoman played by Anne Hathaway, who ends the clip with the line, “You should be as afraid of him as I am.”
The all-star cast also features Marion Cotillard, Michael Caine and Morgan Freeman. (TL)
